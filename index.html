Ashray Introduction

This center provides Sports Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation services to clients in and around Surat, Gujarat. We are experts in the field of Human Movement, Function and Performance and our team of highly experienced Physiotherapists specialise in the assessment and treatment of musculoskeletal injuries. Injuries commonly occur during sports, training and the rigours of everyday life. This leads to pain, disability, poor performance and a reduced quality of life. You certainly do not need to be a sports fanatic to benefit from our services! We aim to give effective treatment to your injury and symptoms, through accurate diagnosis, based on current research, and return you to pain-free function quickly. The identification of predisposing factors also forms an essential part of the overall injury management.

Silent Faetures

Initial sessions usually require a very hands-on approach to assist joints, nerves and muscles to move freely. As a compliment to manual therapy, clients are encouraged to take a central role in their rehabilitation through postural re-education and personalised exercise programmes. This ensures the chances of symptoms returning are minimised.
